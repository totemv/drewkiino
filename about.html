Andrew Aquino

Full-Stack iOS Developer, Entrepreneur, Foodie, and Music enthusiast

Hi, my name is Andrew Aquino.

I am a seasoned and passionate developer. I have worked on over several apps in my career, two of which are live. I am a student at heart, forever learning and increasing my craftsmanship. I have chops in both UX and product management. I am experienced in leading and/or working with colleagues to execute the developmental cycle of mobile applications, as well as software architecture, product planning, ideation, execution, analysis, and iteration. I take into account the overall needs of the business, stakeholders, and of our users in my decisions.

On my spare time, I like to create frameworks that (to the best of my abilities!) make developing easy for everybody. My first one is Atlantis, a logging framework, made for XCode written in Swift. My second one, Whirlpool, is a lightweight iOS chat framework. There are many others in my GitHub. I also made my own Node.JS server that’s being used by many of the production apps I’ve worked with.

Other things about me: I love hiking, camping, going to festivals, listening to music, fitness, watching movies, grabbing a couple mulitas at Gus' Lunchbox, meeting new people, hanging out with friends, and bettering myself as a person!
