Andrew Aquino

Senior Full-Stack iOS Developer, Entrepreneur, Foodie, and Music enthusiast

Hi, my name is Andrew Aquino! I am a Senior Full-Stack iOS Developer. I can make websites, build servers, but I mainly build iOS apps. I may not look like it, but I am very good at what I do. I manage a contract company I founded with a group of friends called TotemV. We mainly create software for businesses, startups, and for personal projects. I have experience in the startup industry, the iOS developer cycle, and creating, building, and managing products. I have released two apps in the Apple store, and I have developed over several start-up apps over the course of my career and have created and managed our production servers for both our own apps and the apps that we do under contract. I have also worked on creating a Virtual Reality game, PLUR-VR, a music festival simulator. It works I guess, I called it Bermuda. Hopefully, if I have more time, I can work on it some more and release it to the public. On my spare time, I like to create frameworks that (to the best of my abilities) make developing easy for everybody. My first one is a logging framework made for XCode written in Swift. I called it Atlantis. My passion is in developing, I find happiness in doing it, especially when it makes other people's lives easier. Software aside, I manage and help grow our contract company by making connections with other businesses. On the side, my team and I do contracts for startups, small businesses, and companies to pay for our bread. It's definitely a load of work but I love doing it.

Other things about me: I go to CSUN, I love hiking, camping, going to festivals, listening to music, going to the gym, watching movies, eating at my favorite taco truck, meeting new people, hanging out with friends, and bettering myself as a person! And I am a sucker for Jollibee, and I can't seem to shake it...
