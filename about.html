Andrew Aquino

Full-Stack iOS Developer, Entrepreneur, Foodie, and Music enthusiast

Hi, my name is Andrew Aquino! I am a Full-Stack iOS Developer. I have experience developing high-scalability production servers for both front-end web and mobile apps using Node.JS.

I have a lot of experience in the startup industry. During the course of my career, I have worked with a handful of startups including successfully starting one of my own called Drew’s List, it has received awards as well as gaining a spot in Facebook’s exclusive bootstrap program, and is slowly growing. I have released two apps in the Apple store as well as having developed two profitable contract apps, PLUR-VR & FoodTruckieLA, within the same year.

All that aside, my bread and butter is iOS development, it is both my passion and my life’s work.

On my spare time, I like to create frameworks that (to the best of my abilities!) make developing easy for everybody. My first one is Atlantis, a logging framework, made for XCode written in Swift. My second one, Whirlpool, is a lightweight iOS chat framework. There are many others in my GitHub. I also made my own Node.JS server that’s being used by many of the production apps I’ve worked with.

Other things about me: I love hiking, camping, going to festivals, listening to music, going to the gym, watching movies, eating at my favorite taco truck, meeting new people, hanging out with friends, and bettering myself as a person! And I am a sucker for Jollibee, my native hometown fast-food chain, and I can't seem to shake it.

I do professional contracts and consultation. Please email me for more info.
